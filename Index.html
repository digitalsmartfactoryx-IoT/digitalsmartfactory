About Digital Smart Factory

Digital Smart Factory helps manufacturing organizations transition from isolated machines to fully connected, data-driven operations using Industry 4.0 and Industrial IoT principles.

We specialize in OPC-based industrial connectivity, enabling seamless integration of CNC machines, PLCs, energy meters, and sensors into a single scalable data platform for production monitoring, energy optimization, and decarbonization initiatives.

OPC-Based Smart IoT Gateway

Our architecture is built around a centralized OPC-based Smart IoT Gateway that enables secure, scalable, and vendor-agnostic connectivity across diverse industrial assets.

Supported CNC & PLC Platforms

  • CNC Controllers: FANUC, Brother, Siemens Sinumerik
  • PLCs: Siemens, Mitsubishi, Delta
  • Energy meters, sensors, drives, and utility equipment

Industrial Protocols & Interfaces

  • OPC UA / OPC DA
  • Modbus RTU (RS-485) & Modbus TCP/IP
  • FOCAS for FANUC CNC connectivity
  • SLMP & MC Protocol for Mitsubishi PLCs
  • Profinet & Profibus
  • TCP/IP, Serial, and custom machine interfaces

Scalability & Architecture

A single Smart IoT Gateway can reliably connect and manage 100–200 machines, acting as a unified data hub for real-time acquisition, normalization, and distribution to MES, EMS, ERP, analytics platforms, or cloud systems.

This approach reduces infrastructure complexity, minimizes licensing overhead, and ensures consistent data governance across the factory.
