OPC-Based Smart IoT Gateway Architecture

Our reference architecture is centered around a centralized Smart OPC IoT Gateway, designed to securely connect diverse shopfloor assets into a standardized and scalable data model.

Supported CNC & PLC Platforms

  • CNC Controllers: FANUC, Brother, Siemens Sinumerik
  • PLC Platforms: Siemens, Mitsubishi, Delta
  • Energy meters, drives, sensors, and industrial utilities

Industrial Protocols & Interfaces

  • OPC UA / OPC DA
  • Modbus RTU (RS-485) & Modbus TCP/IP
  • FOCAS for FANUC CNC connectivity
  • SLMP & MC Protocol for Mitsubishi PLCs
  • Profinet, Profibus, TCP/IP, Serial interfaces

Scalability & Deployment

A single Smart OPC Gateway can reliably connect and manage 100–200 machines, acting as a unified data hub for MES, EMS, ERP, analytics platforms, and cloud systems.

This architecture minimizes infrastructure complexity, reduces licensing overhead, and ensures consistent data governance across large manufacturing plants.
