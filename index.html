FACTORY LINK+

Our Flagship Platform

Factory Link+ is a comprehensive Industrial IoT platform designed to provide real-time visibility across utilities, machines, and manufacturing processes.

Energy & Utilities

Intelligent monitoring of Electrical Panels, DG Sets, Solar Plants, and HVAC/Chiller systems.

  • Peak load alerts
  • Power quality analytics
  • Sustainability reporting

CNC & Machine Viz

Real-time connectivity for FANUC, Siemens, Mitsubishi, and Allen Bradley controllers.
